How to Play Tetris Blitz

  • Move falling pieces left and right to position them on the board.
  • Rotate pieces to fit them into gaps.
  • Complete full rows to clear them and score points.
  • Clear 4 lines at once (a "Tetris") for massive bonus points.
  • Hold a piece to save it for later — press C or Shift.
  • Use hard drop (Space) to instantly place a piece.
  • In Sprint modes, race to clear 40 or 100 lines as fast as possible.
  • In Endless mode, play until the board fills up. Speed increases every 10 lines.

Controls

← → Move left/right
↓ Soft drop
↑ or X Rotate CW
Z Rotate CCW
Space Hard drop
C or Shift Hold piece
P or Esc Pause
Touch: Swipe / Tap
