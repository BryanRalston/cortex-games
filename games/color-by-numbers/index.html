Color By Numbers

Pick an image, choose difficulty, and paint by dragging

How to Play Color By Numbers

  • Pick an image from the gallery or upload your own photo.
  • Choose difficulty — Easy (8 colors), Medium (14), or Hard (22) color categories.
  • Select a color from the palette on the right (or tap on mobile).
  • Drag to paint — click and drag across cells matching your selected color to fill them in.
  • Reveal the image — as you fill cells, the original photo colors emerge as a mosaic.
  • Use Peek (hold) to glimpse the original, Glow to highlight matching cells, and Mag for a magnifier.
  • Press Ctrl+Z to undo, or number keys 1-9 to quickly switch colors.
