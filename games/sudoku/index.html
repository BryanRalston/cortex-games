How to Play

  • Fill the grid so every row, column, and 3×3 box contains 1–9 exactly once.
  • Tap a cell then tap a number (or use keyboard 1–9). Use arrows to move.
  • Tap PENCIL (or hold Shift) to toggle small candidate marks.
  • Red numbers = conflict with another in the same row, column or box.
  • Undo (or Ctrl/Cmd+Z), limited hints, timer, and best times are saved locally.
  • Every puzzle has exactly one solution. Clues are shown in bright text.
