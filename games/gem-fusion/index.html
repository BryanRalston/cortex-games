How to Play Gem Fusion

  • Swap adjacent gems by clicking/tapping one, then clicking/tapping its neighbor — or swipe on mobile.
  • Match 3+ gems of the same color to clear them and score points.
  • Match 4 in a row to create a Line Blast gem that clears an entire row or column.
  • Match 5+ to create a Color Bomb that clears all gems of one color.
  • Combos — cascading matches multiply your score.
  • In Levels mode, reach the goal score within the move limit. In Endless, play for the high score.
  • If no moves are available, the board shuffles automatically.
